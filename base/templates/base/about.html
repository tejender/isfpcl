About ISFPC

The Farmer Producer Company has a set of primary objectives aimed at enhancing the welfare and interests of its members. These objectives include a range of activities such as harvesting, procurement, pooling, production, selling, grading, handling, marketing, and exporting primary produce. Additionally, the company provides technical services, training, education, consultancy, research and development to its members. Another important objective is the revitalization of land and water resources, the generation, transmission, and distribution of power, as well as communications and conservation efforts related to primary produce.

The company also offers machinery, equipment, and consumables to its members. Moreover, it promotes mutual assistance, financial services, insurance of producers, and welfare measures for primary produce. In summary, the Farmer Producer Company aims to provide a comprehensive range of services that support the production and marketing of primary produce for the benefit of its members.
