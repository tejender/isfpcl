About Company

The Farmer Producer Company has a set of primary objectives aimed at enhancing the welfare and interests of its members. These objectives include a range of activities such as harvesting, procurement, pooling, production, selling, grading, handling, marketing, and exporting primary produce. Additionally, the company provides technical services, training, education, consultancy, research and development to its members. Another important objective is the revitalization of land and water resources, the generation, transmission, and distribution of power, as well as communications and conservation efforts related to primary produce.

The company also offers machinery, equipment, and consumables to its members. Moreover, it promotes mutual assistance, financial services, insurance of producers, and welfare measures for primary produce. In summary, the Farmer Producer Company aims to provide a comprehensive range of services that support the production and marketing of primary produce for the benefit of its members.

Business Activity

Farmer Producer Company aims to provide different services to its member-farmers, which includes

Service provider for cold storage

We as a FPC plans to provide cost-effective cold storage services to its farmer members in order to eliminate non-value adding intermediaries from the value chain. This would lead to better price realization for farmers and reduction in wastage particularly during harvest period.

Agricultural Equipments on Rental Basis

The FPC plans to provide agriculture equipments on rental basis to its farmer members at affordable rates. These equipment includes Power Tiller, Power Sprayer , Grass Cutter Power Chain Saw and Earth Auger etc.

facilitation of input suplly

The FPC plans to facilitate input supply to its farmer members through a common interesr group By consolidating demand and precuring input in bulk , the fpc can save on transportation costs and and negotiate better prices from suppliers

Facilitting Collective Marketing

We will facilitate collective marketing of Apple and Persimmion produced by member-farmers, which aims to address the problem of small marketingsurplus and high transportation cost.
